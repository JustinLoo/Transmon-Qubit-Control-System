our whole goal here is to send rf to a transmon. now that we know how to make a transmon in python, we have to 
simulate how to show how to add RF signals

To model this we use the time depdenent schrodinger euqation, where

$$H(t) = H_{\text{static}} + V_{\text{RF}}(t)$$

where $H_{\text{static}}$ is the transmon

$V_{\text{RF}}(t)$ is the microwave pulse


so our full time depdenednt schrodinger equation looks like this:

$$i\hbar \frac{\partial}{\partial t} \psi(\phi, t) = \left[ \underbrace{-4E_C \frac{\partial^2}{\partial \phi^2} - E_J \cos(\phi)}_{\text{Static Chip Hamiltonian } (\hat{H}_0)} + \underbrace{V_{mw}(t) \cdot \left(-i \frac{\partial}{\partial \phi}\right)}_{\text{Microwave Drive interaction}} \right] \psi(\phi, t)$$
